Who We Are

We are specialty coffee roasters, family owned and run. Every roast is carefully handcrafted to bring out the best taste. We only roast 100% Arabica Coffee in small batches. Currently we are roasting on a Dalian Amazon 1kg drum roaster. The roastery was set up in a rarely used storeroom at our house and we used only recycled material to build everything that we needed for the roastery.

The roastmasters are a father & son team (Leon Snr & Leon Jnr). Our social media liason is Gertie Shields (daughter). The naming of our business and design of our labels was a real family effort involving even our cousins in the USA.

We source our beans from around the world. We are currently roasting Ethiopian Sidamo, unwashed, and Brasil Santos, unwashed, coffee beans. We do them as single origin roasts but are in the process of developing a trademark blend to compliment and expand our range. We love our coffee and intend to roast only to the highest quality.
